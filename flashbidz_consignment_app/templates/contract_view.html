3. Commission, Fees & Selling Channels

The Consignor authorizes FlashBidz to sell the items listed under this contract under the following fee structure:

Channel Description Consignor Commission / Fees
FlashBidz Online Auction Items listed and sold through a FlashBidz online auction event. FlashBidz retains a 13% commission on the final hammer price for consigned items sold via auction. Buyer’s premium (if any) is retained by FlashBidz and is not shared with the Consignor.
In-Store Retail Sales Items offered for sale in the FlashBidz storefront or similar in-person retail channels. FlashBidz retains a 25% commission on the final selling price for consigned items sold in the store.
eBay / Online Marketplace Sales Items listed on eBay or similar third-party online marketplaces by FlashBidz. FlashBidz retains a 25% commission on the final selling price plus all actual third-party platform fees, payment processing fees, promoted listing fees, and applicable shipping label costs incurred to sell and deliver the item.
Estate / Full-Service Consignments For full household/estate projects where FlashBidz provides on-site labor, sorting, packing, and marketing services. Unless a different percentage is agreed to in writing on the front of this contract, the standard estate consignment fee is 35% of gross auction proceeds (exclusive of buyer’s premium and sales tax), plus any separately agreed clean-out, disposal, or marketing fees listed in the notes section.

Any different commission rates or flat fees for a specific project, consignor, or item must be written clearly in the “Special Terms / Notes” section of this Agreement and initialed by both parties. If no alternate rate is written, the standard rates above apply.

5. Term, Exclusivity & Price Control

Consigned items remain under the exclusive control of FlashBidz for the duration of this Agreement. The Consignor agrees not to remove, sell, or give away items listed on this contract without first obtaining written permission from FlashBidz. FlashBidz will determine the timing, lot grouping, description, and starting prices at its professional discretion, taking into account the Consignor’s reasonable preferences when possible.

6. Condition, Descriptions & No Guarantee

  • Items are sold “AS IS, WHERE IS”, with no warranties expressed or implied.
  • Descriptions and photos are based on information provided by the Consignor and visual inspection; FlashBidz is not responsible for hidden defects, authenticity of autographs/collectibles, or manufacturer representations.
  • The Consignor affirms that all items are owned outright and are free of liens or undisclosed claims and that no stolen or illegal items are being consigned.

7. Sales Tax, Buyer’s Premium & Payment Processing

FlashBidz will collect applicable sales tax, buyer’s premium, and payment processing fees from buyers as required. These amounts are retained by FlashBidz and/or remitted to the taxing authorities and are not included in the Consignor’s share.

8. Payouts to Consignor

  • Unless otherwise agreed in writing, FlashBidz will issue payment to the Consignor within 30 days after the close of the auction or sale period, once buyer payments have cleared.
  • Payment may be made by check, cash, or electronic payment method at FlashBidz’s discretion.
  • FlashBidz may withhold payouts for unresolved chargebacks, non-paying bidders, returned items, or disputes relating to the consigned items until the issue is resolved.

9. Pickup, Storage & Abandoned Property

  • Winning bidders are generally allowed 7 days from the end of the auction or sale to pick up purchased items, unless a different pickup window is advertised.
  • After 7 days, FlashBidz may charge buyers a storage fee of $2.00 per item per day. These fees are retained by FlashBidz and are not shared with the Consignor.
  • If a buyer fails to pay or pick up an item, FlashBidz may cancel the sale, re-sell the item, or dispose of it at its discretion. FlashBidz is not required to pay the Consignor for items that were never fully paid for by a buyer.
  • Unpaid or unclaimed items that cannot be re-sold within a reasonable period may be considered abandoned and may be donated, discarded, or otherwise disposed of by FlashBidz without further notice to the Consignor.

10. Unsold Items

FlashBidz and the Consignor will discuss how to handle unsold items after the primary sale period. Options may include:

  • Re-listing in a future auction or sale;
  • Moving to in-store retail or online marketplaces at the applicable commission rate;
  • Donation to charity; or
  • Return to the Consignor at the Consignor’s expense.

Unless otherwise stated in writing, items left with FlashBidz more than 30 days after the end of the last scheduled sale for those items may be treated as abandoned and may be donated or disposed of at FlashBidz’s discretion.

11. Limitation of Liability

  • Reasonable care will be taken with consigned items; however, normal handling wear, shelf wear, and minor damage may occur in the ordinary course of moving and displaying items.
  • FlashBidz is not responsible for loss or damage caused by fire, theft, water, acts of God, or circumstances beyond its reasonable control.
  • In any event, FlashBidz’s maximum liability, if any, shall not exceed the amount that would have been payable to the Consignor as net proceeds for the affected item(s) if they had been sold under this Agreement.

12. Disputes & Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Michigan. Any disputes arising out of this Agreement shall be handled first through good-faith discussions between the parties. If unresolved, the parties agree that any claims may be brought in a court of competent jurisdiction located in or near Kalamazoo County, Michigan, unless otherwise required by law.

13. Entire Agreement

This document, together with any attached schedules and written addenda initialed by both parties, is the entire Agreement between the Consignor and FlashBidz regarding the items listed under this contract number. Any changes must be in writing and signed or initialed by both parties.
